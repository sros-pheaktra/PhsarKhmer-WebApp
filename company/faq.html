About Us

1. What does “Phsar Khmer” mean?

In Khmer, “Phsar” means market and “Khmer” refers to the Cambodian people. Together, it means Khmer Market, a place where locals buy and sell daily goods.

2. What can I find at a Phsar Khmer?

You’ll see a mix of fresh fruits, vegetables, fish, meat, spices, household items, clothing, and even souvenirs.

3. Who shops at Phsar Khmer?

Mostly locals shop there for daily needs, but tourists often visit to experience Cambodian culture, try traditional food, and buy crafts.

4. Is the food safe to eat?

Street food is popular and usually safe if freshly cooked. Look for busy stalls with many customers for the best and safest options.

5. Why are Phsar Khmer markets important?

They are not just shopping places but also social spaces where people meet, share stories, and keep Cambodian traditions alive.
